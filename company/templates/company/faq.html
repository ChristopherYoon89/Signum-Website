FAQ

The Frequently Asked Questions section covers formal topics such as account access or general information about the platform and provides clear answers to common administrative and technical questions.

For best practices and guidance on how to use the interface, please refer to the User Guidelines, which provide more detailed usage instructions. If you are looking for guidance on how to access our public API, we refer to the API page or our API documentation.

What content is published on Signum News?

Signum News extracts and publishes a wide range of different information from thousands of sources worldwide. The majority of our content consists of English-language news and analysis covering current events and general topics. As we prioritize linking content that is not behind paywalls, certain media outlets are less likely part of our source pool.

In addition to news articles, we publish links to commentaries, podcasts, blog posts, analytical articles, statistics and in rare cases market reports or even datasets. Signum News extracts news articles and analysis not only from traditional mainstream media outlets, but also from independent journalists, online media and blogs.

The news is organized into more than 15 categories, including regional categories that primarily focus on political developments, as well as categories focusing on money & finance, companies, commodities, Bitcoin, science, technology or culture. For a detailed list of categories and their definitions check out our user guidelines.

Is Signum News free to use or do I need a subscription?

In general, Signum News and basic functionalities are free to use. However, we plan to roll out a subscription based business version that offers an expanded set of tools and functionalities. The business version will also include a data analysis dashboard, automated reporting features as well as enhanced exporting tools.

Who uses our news service?

Signum News is designed for both individual and private users and businesses and organizations. Individual users are often dissatisfied with the current media landscape and are seeking alternative topics, sources and perspectives. Most of our readers identify with the liberal-conservative and libertarian spectrum. However, our audience also includes pragmatic thinkers, quantitative analysts, engineers, Bitcoiners, contrarians, rationalists or realists. In general, we do not discriminate against any group and invite everyone with a critical and open mind to use our platform.

The information published by Signum News places high demands on our readers. Our audience is used to engaging with dense information and a wide range of opinions. If you are looking for propaganda, gossip or other superficial content, you are most likely in the wrong place.

Signum News can be also used by businesses and other organizations at a professional level. The dashboard allows users to completely control and customize their information streams. Users can create feeds and customize their algorithms and filters according to their individual needs. Moreover, bookmark lists enable users to conduct extensive and focused research and manage their bookmarks and research projects. For businesses we also provide access to our public API and data analysis tools in the business version.

How often is new content published?

Our data crawler extracts thousands of news articles on a daily basis, which are then processed and transformed into a high-quality dataset. Between 100 and 150 articles are uploaded to the website each day.

In which languages are the news articles available?

Right now, articles are published in English. In general, our language detection algorithms are able to filter out non-English articles with a relatively high precision. However, in rare cases articles in other languages might get mixed into our news pool.

Is it legal to crawl and aggregate news or does it violate copy rights or intellectual property rights?

According to our legal experts, our service does not violate copy rights and intellectual property rights as we do not publish the full texts or summaries of the articles. In addition to this, we link each article to the original source and provide the name of the source as citation.

Most media outlets are thankful for the traffic we generate from our platform to their websites. However, if you want to remove your news outlet from the Signum News platform, you can send an inquiry via our contact form.

How can I contact customer service?

For quick inquiries, you can reach us via our contact form. For a more formal correspondence, you may send us a letter to our business address.

Further questions

I forgot my password. What can I do?

Click on “Forgot password” on the login page, enter your email address and select “Reset password.” You will receive an automatically generated email that allows you to reset your password. Please check your spam folder if you do not receive the email. If you have forgotten both your password and your email address, please contact us via our contact form. Further instructions will then be sent by email.

How can I change my profile information?

Users can update their personal information, login details and profile description in their profile settings. For security reasons we ask our users to confirm profile changes by providing their password.

Why was my account suspended?

We only suspend accounts in cases of serious violations of our terms of use or applicable law. In principle, we try to avoid suspensions whenever possible. If your account was suspended without justification or due to a technical error, please contact us immediately via our contact form.

What happens to my data?

By using our website, you agree to our terms and conditions and privacy policy. We reserve the right to retain user data for up to six months after account or subscription cancellation, after which it is deleted. Generally speaking, we do not have an interest in storing or monetizing your personal and financial data. We fully comply with applicable data protection laws as defined by the EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU Regulation 2016/679). For more information about our data privacy policy, we refer to our privacy page.

Can the service also be accessed on mobile devices?

The startpage of the website is fully usable via mobile browsers and optimized for smartphones and tablets. However, Signum News is a complex businesses software, which is why we are currently not able to provide a flawless usage and full functionality in the mobile browser. As a business software the platform is supposed to be used on the desktop PC, laptops or tablets. For mobile users we are currently developing a dedicated mobile app, which will be optimized for mobile requirements and is expected to be released later this year.

Is it possible to include my media outlet in the aggregator?

Yes, we are generally open to such requests. You may submit your media outlet/source via our contact form. However, each request will be carefully reviewed by our editorial team before being added to the source pool. Please note that we do not accept payments for inclusion, nor can we guarantee that articles from your outlet will be published on Signum News. Our algorithms evaluate each link independently based on the quality and relevance of the article before it becomes part of our feed.

However, we may reserve the right to publish sponsored articles in dedicated areas clearly declared as sponsored content. If you are interested in publishing a sponsored article in dedicated areas, you can reach us via our contact form.
