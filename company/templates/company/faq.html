FAQ

The Frequently Asked Questions section covers formal topics such as subscriptions, account access and general information about the platform. It is intended to provide clear answers to common administrative and technical questions.

For guidance on how to use the interface, configure feeds or work with filtering options, please refer to the User Guidelines, which provides more detailed usage instructions. If you are looking for the API documentation, see here.

What content is published on Signum News?

Signum News extracts and publishes a wide range of different information from thousands of sources worldwide. The majority of our content consists of English-language news from covering global events and other topics. As we prioritize linking content that is not behind paywalls, certain media outlets are not part of our source pool.

In addition to news articles, we publish links to commentaries, podcasts, blog posts, analytical articles, statistics and in rare cases market reports or even datasets.

The news is organized into more than 15 categories, including regional categories that primarily focus on political developments, as well as categories covering money & finance, companies, science, technology or culture.

What are the benefits of a subscription?

In general, Signum News is free to use. However, we plan to roll out a business version that offers an expanded set of tools and functionalities. With a subscription, users gain an exclusive access to the full news archive as well as additional tools. The subscription includes a data analysis dashboard, automated reporting features as well as enhanced exporting tools. Of course, all advertising banners are hidden for subscribers.

How much does a subscription cost?

Further details regarding pricing for the business software will be announced once the analysis tool is ready for use.

Can the subscription be cancelled at any time?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. For annual subscriptions, you will be notified by email 14 days before the subscription expires. If a subscription is not cancelled, it will be automatically renewed.

You can cancel your subscription with a single click in your profile settings under the “Subscription” section.

What payment options are available?

Signum News uses Stripe for payment processing, which allows users to pay either by credit/debit card or with a stripe account. A Stripe account is not required. We are also working on a Bitcoin payment solution, which is expected to be available in the coming months.

I forgot my password. What can I do?

Click on “Forgot password” on the login page, enter your email address and select “Reset password.” You will receive an automatically generated email that allows you to reset your password. Please check your spam folder if you do not receive the email. If you have forgotten both your password and your email address, please contact us via the contact form. Further instructions will then be sent by email.

How can I contact customer service?

For quick inquiries, you can reach us via our contact form. For a more formal correspondence, you may also send us a letter to our business address.

Further Questions

How often is new content published?

Our data crawler extracts thousands of news articles on a daily basis, which are then processed and transformed into a high-quality dataset. Between 100 and 150 news articles are uploaded to the website each day.

In which languages are the news articles available?

Most articles are published in English. All content meets our high quality standards and aims to provide balanced, comprehensive coverage of relevant topics.

Who uses our news service?

Signum News is designed for readers who are dissatisfied with the current media landscape and are seeking a broader range of topics, sources and perspectives. Most of our readers identify with the liberal-conservative and libertarian spectrum. However, our audience also includes pragmatic thinkers, quantitative analysts, Bitcoiners, contrarians, rationalists or realists.

The information published by Signum News places high demands on its readers. Our audience is used to engaging with dense information and a wide range of opinions. If you are looking for propaganda, gossip or other superficial content, you are most likely in the wrong place.

Can the service also be accessed on mobile devices?

Generally speaking, the website is fully usable via mobile browsers and optimized for smartphones and tablets. Due to the complexity of the platform and its features, we are currently developing a dedicated mobile app, which is expected to be released later this year. Until then, all functionality remains available via the mobile web version.

Is it possible to try the subscription for free?

No, Signum News does not offer a free trial. Free trials are frequently abused by bots attempting to scrape our data. Additionally, VPN services make it easy to repeatedly create trial accounts. That said, part of our content is freely accessible, allowing you to get an initial impression. You can also watch our product videos, which showcase exclusive content and features. Additionally, users may start with a monthly subscription before committing to an annual plan.

What happens to my data?

By using our website, you agree to our terms and conditions and privacy policy. We reserve the right to retain user data for up to six months after subscription cancellation, after which it is deleted. We fully comply with applicable data protection laws as defined by the EU General Data Protection Regulation (EU Regulation 2016/679).

Why was my account suspended?

We only suspend accounts in cases of serious violations of our terms of use or applicable law. In principle, we try to avoid suspensions whenever possible. If your account was suspended without justification or due to a technical error, please contact us immediately via the contact form.

How can I change my profile information?

User can update their personal information, login details and profile description in their profile settings.

Is it possible to include my media outlet in the aggregator?

Yes, we are generally open to such requests. You may submit your media outlet/source via the contact form. However, each request will be carefully reviewed by our editorial team before being added to the source pool. Please note that we do not accept payments for inclusion, nor can we guarantee that articles from your outlet will be published on Signum News. Our algorithms evaluate each link independently based on the quality and relevance of the article before it becomes part of our feed.
