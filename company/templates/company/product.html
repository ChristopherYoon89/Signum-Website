Hey there! My name is Chris, I am a 37 years old programmer and I am running one of the world's largest databases with news articles, analysis, blog posts and other content. Every night and day my servers crawl thousands of sources, transform raw data into structured high quality datasets and update my databases and apps.

Signum News provides a graphical user interface to interact with my database in various ways, such as to search, explore, filter or analyze articles or to create and manage personal feeds and custom briefings. One of the central themes of Signum is to filter the signal from the noise and help users focus on relevant, high-quality information and knowledge.

Signum News offers a wide range of tools to explore and interact with information. These tools can be used in multiple ways and are build for both individual and private users, but also for businesses and organizations. You can

  • filter news across more than 15 categories including politics in different geographic regions, economic topics, tech or culture.
  • customize your own briefing and feeds based on categories, keywords, sources or quantitative metrics.
  • bookmark articles and organize your bookmarks through bookmark lists for specific research topics.
  • retrieve detailed statistics on articles, sources and user signals, including user ratings, source ratings, algorithm ratings or clicks count.
  • search for articles in our archive based on keywords and other search parameters.
  • export your data as csv or JSON files for your research or personal workflows.
  • access our API for a deeper integration of news data.
