One of the central themes of Signum is to filter the signal from the noise, helping users focus on relevant, high-quality information. Signum News provides two core functionalities:

  • First, it aggregates and curates news and other content from thousands of sources around the world.
  • Second, the platform allows users to create their own feeds and customize their personal algorithm using categories, keywords, sources or quality indicators.

In addition to aggregated news and customized feeds, Signum News offers a wide range of tools to explore and interact with information. Users can

  • filter news across more than 15 categories including politics in different geographic regions, economic topics, tech or culture.
  • customize their own briefing and feeds based on categories, keywords, sources or quantitative metrics.
  • bookmark articles and organize their bookmarks through bookmark feeds for specific research topics.
  • retrieve detailed statistics on articles, sources and user signals, including user ratings, source ratings, algorithm rating or clicks count.
  • search for articles in our archive based on keywords and other search parameters.
  • export their data as csv or JSON files.
  • access our API for a deeper integration of news data and their own software and use our endpoints, filter fields or ordering fields.
