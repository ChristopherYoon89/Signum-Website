Sponsoring

Signum News offers businesses the opportunity to sponsor selected areas of the platform. Sponsoring allows companies to present their brand, logo or specific products in a dedicated section on the start page. These placements are designed to be clearly visible while remaining separate from editorial content.

Sponsored placements are limited to clearly defined areas of the website and are intended to provide relevant visibility for organizations whose products or services align with the interests of our users. Sponsoring does not influence article selection, categorization, rankings or any other editorial or algorithmic processes on the platform.

Companies interested in sponsoring opportunities can contact us directly via the contact form. Please include basic information about your company, the type of placement you are interested in and any relevant details about your brand or product. We will review your request and provide further information about availability, formats and conditions.

All sponsoring arrangements are subject to review to ensure they are appropriate for the platform and its audience.
