Donate in Bitcoin

This website provides free access to news data and useful tools that help people stay informed in an increasingly noisy world. Even though we plan a commercial business version in the future, there are no paywalls for the basic functionalities and the goal is to keep information open and accessible to everyone.

If you find value in this platform and want to support its continued development, you can make a voluntary donation. Contributions help cover infrastructure, development and maintenance. Donations are accepted via Bitcoin and Bitcoin Lightning.

Apart from volutary donations, organizations and individuals can also support the project by becoming a sponsor. Sponsors will have their logo displayed on the start page as a way to show their support for open access to information. If you are interested in sponsoring rather than donating, contact us for further information.

Thank you for helping keep free information alive.
