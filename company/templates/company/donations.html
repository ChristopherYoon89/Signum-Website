Signum News provides free access to news data and tools that help people stay informed in an increasingly noisy world. We heavily benefit from public, free and open source information, but our data and platforms are also free to use.

At the same time, maintaining a global content database, integrated data pipelines and the Signum platform requires hardware infrastructure, data processing, ongoing development and maintenance. Servers, data pipelines and engineering all create real operational costs.

Even though we plan a commercial business version in the future, there are no paywalls for the basic functionalities and the goal is to keep information open and accessible to everyone.

If you find value in this platform and want to support its continued development, you can make a voluntary donation. Contributions help cover infrastructure, development and maintenance.

Apart from voluntary donations, organizations and individuals can also support the project by becoming a sponsor. Sponsors will have their logo displayed on the start page as a way to show their support. If you are interested in sponsoring rather than donating, contact us for further information.

Thank you for helping keep free information alive.
