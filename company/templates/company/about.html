About Signum News

One of the central themes of Signum is to filter the signal from the noise, helping users focus on relevant, high-quality information. Signum News provides two core functionalities:

  • First, it aggregates and curates news and other content from thousands of sources around the world.
  • Second, the platform allows users to create their own feeds and customize their personal algorithm using categories, keywords, sources or quality indicators.

In addition to aggregated news and customized feeds, Signum News offers a wide range of tools to explore and interact with information. Users can

  • customize not only their feeds but also their daily briefing
  • filter news across more than 15 categories including geographic regions, economic topics, tech or culture
  • refine their feed and research by sources or keywords
  • bookmark articles
  • follow sources
  • retrieve detailed statistics on articles, sources and user signals
  • search for articles in our archive
  • export data
  • access our API for a deeper integration of news data and their own software

The website is completely free to use. Signum News benefits heavily from open-source data and open knowledge. While a commercial business software version is planned for the future, the public website will remain free and open.

How it Started

Signum is the result of one of my side projects that was originally initiated by a financial expert with a strong vision: provide insights on current events and other topics from a broad range of sources by using modern digital technology and AI. After more than a year of development, however, the project was abandoned as the scope grew too large and the project team lost focus and motivation.

Instead of letting the code rot on a local machine, I decided to roll out the project independently without the initial project team. One of the key lessons I learned from the failure of the original project was to focus on the core ideas and functionalities, rather than getting lost by the never ending influx of new ideas and unnecessary embellishments.

Our Vision, Values and Goals

Signum News empowers users to access high quality information products and services around the world. The platform is designed to help users filter the signal from the noise and focus on high-signal information.

The long-term goal of Signum News is to become a leading provider of high-quality information and high-signal content. Beyond content itself, Signum News seeks to serve as a role model for digital innovation and modern technology, demonstrating how robust systems and thoughtful design can coexist. Ultimately, our broader ambition is to contribute to the development of a freer and more open society.

At the core of Signum News lies a culture centered around genuine value creation. Customer satisfaction is essential, but our aim goes further: to inspire trust and fascination through a combination of quality, user experience and functionality. Guided by a long-term perspective and a low-time-preference mindset, we favor sustainable progress over short-term optimization. Our values are rooted in the principles of life, liberty and property, and we openly support Bitcoin as a symbol of monetary integrity and permissionless innovation.

Organizational Culture

The foundation of Signum News involves a culture built around values such as quality, innovation, efficiency, trust, cooperation and participation, security and resilience as well as life, liberty and property.

To us, entrepreneurship is more important than management. Management is rules-driven, reactive and bureaucratic, while entrepreneurship is visionary, proactive and creative. In other words, we embrace engineers and entrepreneurs, not bureaucrats.

Our business processes are designed to enable experimentation, scientific discovery and an open mind for improvement, out-of-the-box thinking and technology. In doing so, we develop technologies, not only products. But technologies require a rigorous implementation and a sound product strategy. Technologies are also used to make business and production processes more efficient.

Quality is paramount. There is no doubt that branding and marketing matter, but key are the product, its quality and the strategic position of the company. Signum rejects high time preference marketing and branding such as clickbait, scams etc.

Technology & Data

Let's face it, Signum News does not consist of journalists or editors, it is a full stack developing software and data company. Products and apps are based on software and data, processes are based on software and scaling is only possible through software and the heavy use of data science and AI technologies. Learning has a high priority and open source is considered as a production factor.

In addition to the innovative software culture, Signum News heavily relies on conventional engineering principles. The practical engineering culture and its rigor compensates for the creative software culture and its sloppiness. We build robust and scalable systems that need to be integrated. The incorporation of traditional engineering principles into our development process means that we think about the integration of systems and how they interact or that we use first principles to solve problems. Moreover, we constantly evaluate how to deal with certain trade-offs.

On the backend, we operate a data crawler that continuously collects raw URLs from news articles, analysis pieces, blog posts and similar sources. A data pipeline then transforms this raw data into a high-quality dataset, enriching it with structured information such as:

  • Source metadata
  • Keywords and categories
  • Article titles
  • Publication dates
  • External references

On the frontend, we use JavaScript to build dynamic, clearly structured interfaces that are intuitive, easy to use and largely self-explanatory.
