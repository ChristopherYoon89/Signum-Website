About Signum News

One of the central themes of Signum News is to filter the signal from the noise and help users focus on relevant, high-quality information. Signum News provides two core functionalities:

  • It aggregates and curates news and other content from thousands of sources around the world.
  • The platform allows users to create their own feeds and customize their personal algorithm using categories, keywords, sources, quality indicators or user signals.

In addition to aggregated news and customized feeds, Signum News offers a wide range of tools to explore and interact with information. You can

  • filter news across more than 15 categories including geographic regions, economic topics, tech or culture
  • customize your personal feeds and optimize daily briefings according to your individual needs
  • streamline and filter content based on categories, sources, keywords or quantitative indicators
  • bookmark articles and add them to your personal bookmark lists
  • follow sources
  • retrieve detailed statistics on articles, sources, quality indicators and user signals
  • search for articles in our archive
  • export data
  • access our API for a deeper integration of news data and your own software

Signum News benefits heavily from open-source data and open knowledge, but we also share our data, information and knowledge for free. While a commercial business software version is planned for the future, the public website will remain free and open.

How it Started

Signum News is the outcome of one of many side projects that was originally initiated by a friend with a strong vision: provide insights on current events and other topics from a broad range of sources at a time when legacy media outlets have increasingly lost trust and support. After more than a year of development, however, the project was quietly abandoned as the scope grew too large and the original project team lost motivation.

A year later, instead of letting the code rot on a local machine, I decided to roll out the project independently. One of the key lessons I learned from the failure of the original project was to focus on the core ideas and main functionalities, rather than getting lost by the never ending influx of new ideas and embellishments.

Rolling out the project independently was huge, but it allowed me to start from scratch, restructure the platform and focus on strong main functionalities. I built a completely new website with a dynamic interface on the front-end and robust web crawlers and data pipelines on the back-end.

Since then I crawled hundreds of thousands of public and open source news articles and other content, building one of the largest high-quality news articles databases in the world. Around this database I develop different free and commercial software tools and digital services for web applications and mobile apps, Signum News is one of it.

Vision, Values and Goals

The primary vision of Signum News is to empower users to access high-quality information products and services around the world. The platform is designed to help users access a broad range of information and knowledge, but at the same time filter the signal from the noise.

The long-term goal of Signum News is to become a leading provider of high-quality information and high-signal content. Beyond content itself, Signum News seeks to serve as a role model for digital innovation, but also for building robust systems based on sound engineering principles. Ultimately, our broader ambition is to contribute to the emergence of a free and open society, which is why we believe in values such as life, liberty and property.

There is no doubt that branding and marketing matter, but the quality of our product is paramount. Signum News goes to great lengths to develop thoughtful designs and functionalities based on user needs. We strongly believe in innovation and building technologies and tools, not only products. In doing so, we embrace engineering and entrepreneurship rather than bureaucracy and management.

But in addition to innovation and functionality, quality also implies the development of secure and robust systems. Thus, Signum News also values sound engineering principles such as rigor, precision, efficiency and stability. We avoid high time preference thinking and content such as clickbait or scams and focus on the long-term.

Organizational Culture

Signum News combines and balances two technical cultures:

  • the software culture and its focus on functionality, design, creativity and innovation
  • the classical engineering culture and its rigor, precision, efficiency and focus on sound engineering principles such as complex systems thinking, analysis of trade-offs or thinking from first principles.

Technology & Data

Signum News does not consist of journalists or editors, it is a full-stack developing software and data company. Products and production processes are based on software and data and scaling is only possible through the heavy use of data science and AI technologies. Learning has a high priority and open source is considered as a production factor.

On the backend, we operate a data crawler that continuously collects raw URLs from news articles, analysis pieces, blog posts and similar sources. A data pipeline then transforms this raw data into a high-quality dataset by cleaning, structuring and enrichting the data. The final dataset of the crawler and pipeline consists of variables such as

  • URL
  • Title
  • Source
  • Author
  • Publication date
  • Raw text
  • Tags
  • Categories
  • Language
  • Quality ranking Score
